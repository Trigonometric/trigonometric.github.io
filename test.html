In that case は is read as "WA", not "HA" since it is a grammatical particle. Examples:
  • 日本は国です - Japan is a country
  • 鈴木さんは先生です - Mr. Suzuki is a teacher
  • 木村さんは医者です - Mr. Kimura is a doctor
